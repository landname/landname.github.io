About

I'm Gie, a researcher based in Fairfax, VA. My current work focuses on predicting workplace injury and fatality risk across Virginia in collaboration with the Virginia Department of Labor and Industry, combining OSHA enforcement data, Census data, and geospatial methods. More broadly, I'm interested in how machine learning and NLP can turn messy public data into decisions that matter — from government AI use-case inventories to historical urban change measured through 1930s redlining maps.
